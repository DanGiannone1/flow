# Filing Calendar — 2026 (Calendar-Year C Corporation)

Standard federal deadlines for a calendar-year C corporation, for reference when
planning filings and estimated payments.

| Filing | Due date |
|---|---|
| Form 1120 — Federal income tax return | April 15 |
| Form 7004 — Automatic extension (extends to Oct 15) | April 15 |
| Estimated payment — Q1 | April 15 |
| Estimated payment — Q2 | June 15 |
| Estimated payment — Q3 | September 15 |
| Estimated payment — Q4 | December 15 |
| Form 1120 — Extended due date | October 15 |

State returns (e.g. California Form 100) follow their own due dates. Confirm any
weekend/holiday shifts for the specific year.
