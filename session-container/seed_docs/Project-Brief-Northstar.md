# Project Brief — Northstar

## Summary
Northstar is an internal initiative to consolidate our three reporting dashboards into a
single self-serve workspace. The goal is to cut the time teams spend assembling weekly
status reports and to give everyone one trusted view of progress.

## Goals
- Replace the three legacy dashboards with one unified workspace by end of Q3.
- Reduce weekly reporting effort by at least half.
- Make every metric traceable to its source so numbers are trusted, not questioned.

## Out of scope
- Real-time streaming data (batch refresh every 15 minutes is acceptable for v1).
- Mobile-native apps (the responsive web view covers the demo need).

## Milestones
- M1 — Data model and source connectors agreed.
- M2 — Single dashboard shell with one live metric.
- M3 — All three legacy dashboards retired.

## Success metric
A team can produce its weekly status update in under ten minutes using only the workspace.
