# Weekly Review — Standard Operating Procedure

A short, repeatable routine for closing out the week and setting up the next one.
Block 30 minutes every Friday afternoon.

## Steps
1. **Clear the inbox of tasks.** Review every task marked "To do" or "In progress" and
   confirm its status, priority, and due date are still accurate.
2. **Triage what's overdue.** Anything past its due date either gets done, rescheduled with
   a new due date, or dropped. Nothing stays silently overdue.
3. **Check next week's calendar.** Make sure every meeting has a purpose; decline or shorten
   the ones that don't. Block focus time before it fills up.
4. **Pick the top three.** Choose the three tasks that matter most for next week and mark
   them High priority. Everything else is secondary.
5. **Write the status update.** One short paragraph: what moved, what's blocked, what's next.

## Principles
- A task without a clear next step is not a task — it's a note. Break it down or delete it.
- Reschedule honestly. Moving a due date is fine; pretending it isn't overdue is not.
